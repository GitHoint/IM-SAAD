About Us:

national network of library branches spread across all cities and towns in England, providing a wide range of media services, including books, journals, periodicals, CDs, DVDs, games, and multimedia titles

AML operates under a central governance structure with its headquarters located in London, which oversees budget management and overall strategy. Each city or town has a main branch, with larger cities like Sheffield and Manchester having multiple branches to meet the demand, while smaller towns may operate with just one. Within each city, resources such as books and media are shared among branches and managed by the primary branch to ensure efficient utilization.
